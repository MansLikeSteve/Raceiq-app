PRIVACY
POLICY

Last updated: March 2026 · Station8.AI

The short version: We collect only what we need to run the app. We never sell your data. You can delete your account and all your data at any time.

1. Who We Are

Station8.AI ("we", "us", "our") is a Hyrox race intelligence platform. Our website is station8.ai and you can contact us at hello@station8.ai.

2. What Data We Collect

Information you give us directly

  • Email address — when you join the waitlist or create an account
  • Name, city, and profile details — when you set up your profile
  • Race results and split times — when you log races in the app
  • Community posts, comments, and likes — when you engage in the feed
  • Device connection data — when you link Garmin, Strava, or other fitness platforms

Information collected automatically

  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • IP address (used for security and fraud prevention only)
  • Cookies for keeping you signed in

Information from third parties

  • If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email from Google
  • If you connect Strava or Garmin, we receive activity data you authorise

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the Station8.AI service
  • To personalise your race strategy predictions
  • To send you email updates about your account and beta access (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • To display your results in the community feed if you choose to share them
  • To analyse aggregate usage trends to improve the product
  • To prevent fraud and keep the platform secure

4. Who We Share Data With

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with:

  • Supabase — our database and authentication provider (EU servers)
  • Vercel — our hosting provider
  • Stripe — payment processing for Pro subscriptions (they never see your race data)
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — for AI coaching features (only anonymised race data is sent, never your name or email)

We may share data with law enforcement if required by law.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Supabase's EU-based infrastructure. We use Row Level Security (RLS) meaning your private data is only accessible to you. All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are based in the UK or EU, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Rectification — correct any inaccurate data
  • Erasure — delete your account and all associated data
  • Portability — export your race data as a CSV
  • Objection — opt out of marketing emails at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@station8.ai and we will respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies only — to keep you signed in and remember your preferences. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can clear cookies at any time through your browser settings.

8. Children's Privacy

Station8.AI is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has submitted data to us, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the product grows. We will notify registered users by email of any significant changes. The date at the top of this page always shows when it was last updated.

10. Contact

For any privacy questions or requests: privacy@station8.ai

General contact: hello@station8.ai
